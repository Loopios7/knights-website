Joining Process

There are a few steps a joinee needs to complete to be granted access to our player group and squadron. Please follow the below steps.

Squadron

Send in an application to join our ingame squadron, Knights of Karma. The ingame squadron roster is our primary member list.

INARA

Make a join request for our squadron in INARA. INARA is a great resource and can greatly facilitate communication.

Website

Click Sign Up in this website and fill up the form.

Your join request will be accepted as soon as possible and you will be given an invite link to our private Discord server. If you wish to communicate with us in the meantime, please use one of our other channels as mentioned in Contact.
